## Supported Providers

- **OpenAI**
  Use any OpenAI-compatible model (e.g., GPT-4, GPT-4o).

- **Google**
  Use Gemini models and other Google LLM endpoints.

- **Anthropic**
  Use Claude family models (Claude 3, etc.).

---

Need more flexibility? Check out [Custom Providers](./custom-providers.md) to connect your own endpoints.

If you’d like support for a provider that isn’t available yet, [contact our support team](mailto:hi@asserto.ai).
